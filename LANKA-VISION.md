# LankaPros Vision — The Sri Lankan Global Brain Trust

## The Opportunity

**2M+ Sri Lankans live abroad** across 40+ countries:
- Saudi Arabia: 600K | UAE: 300K | Kuwait: 300K
- Canada: 139K | UK: 132K | Australia: 110K | Italy: 110K
- France: 150K | Qatar: 145K | USA: ~50K

**Key professional sectors:**
- Healthcare & Nursing (UK, NZ, Australia, Canada, Middle East)
- IT & Software Engineering (USA, Canada, UK, Australia)
- Engineering (civil, mechanical — Middle East construction)
- Finance & Accounting (UK, Singapore, UAE)
- Hospitality & Tourism (Middle East, Maldives)
- Education & Academia (global)
- Entrepreneurship (growing — diaspora investing back home)

**Remittances = 9.1% of Sri Lanka's GDP** — this diaspora is economically critical.

## What Makes LankaPros Different from LinkedIn

LinkedIn is generic. LankaPros is purpose-built for:

1. **Diaspora Reconnection** — Sri Lankans scattered across 40 countries finding each other
2. **Build Sri Lanka Together** — Projects, investments, mentorship flowing back home
3. **Industry Hubs by Country** — "Sri Lankan nurses in UK" is a community, not a search result
4. **Bilingual** — Sinhala + English (Tamil future roadmap)
5. **Economic Dashboard** — Real-time Sri Lankan economy data (rupee, stocks, remittances)
6. **Project Collaboration** — Not just networking, actually BUILDING together
7. **Mentorship Matching** — Senior diaspora pros matched with young Sri Lankan talent
8. **Job Board** — Curated for Sri Lankan-friendly employers worldwide

## Core Modules (LinkedIn-inspired, Lanka-specific)

### 1. Feed (Home)
- Posts with text/images
- Industry-filtered feed (Healthcare, IT, Engineering, etc.)
- "Trending in the Lankan community" sidebar
- Sri Lankan economic indicators widget

### 2. Profile
- Professional profile (experience, education, skills)
- Customizable themes (MySpace-style — unique differentiator)
- "Countries lived/worked in" map widget
- Analytics: profile views, post impressions
- "Open to" badges: Work, Mentoring, Investing, Collaborating

### 3. Jobs
- Job listings from Sri Lankan-friendly employers
- Country-specific job boards (UK nursing, Middle East engineering, etc.)
- Visa/work permit guidance per country
- Salary benchmarks for Sri Lankans abroad

### 4. Network
- Connections with "mutual connections"
- "Sri Lankans near you" (geo-based discovery)
- Invitations & connection requests
- "People who viewed your profile"

### 5. Groups (Industry Hubs)

- Country chapters: "Sri Lankans in Canada", "Sri Lankans in UAE"
- Project collaboration spaces within groups

### 6. Messaging
- Real-time DMs (Supabase Realtime)

- File sharing for project collaboration

### 7. Projects (UNIQUE to LankaPros)
- "I'm building X for Sri Lanka — who wants to join?"
- Project boards with tasks, members, updates
- Connects diaspora talent with homeland opportunities

### 8. Mentorship (UNIQUE to LankaPros)

### 9. Economy Dashboard
- Live LKR exchange rates
